Now You See Me

By Ivanna

 

About the Album

Fake Smiles Don’t Fool Me is a bold reflection of hidden struggles and strength, showing that masks and false laughter can’t hide the truth. Among its powerful tracks, Now You See Me shines as an anthem of transformation and confidence, proving that the world can no longer ignore the light within.

About the Author

Ivanna Ifesemen is a Nigerian 12-year-old girl who took the world by storm when she released her first album. She started her music career at a very young age but with the disapproval of her siblings she cast it aside. But when she started singing again she got approval from her peers she decided that she wasn't going to let anyone decide her fate, so she started singing again and with great talent. She even got third in her school's talent show
